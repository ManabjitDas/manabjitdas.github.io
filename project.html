Tesla Coil

Our project, the Tesla Coil, is a high-voltage transformer designed to produce impressive electrical discharges. It demonstrates the principles of electromagnetic fields and wireless power transmission. The coil generates dramatic sparks and can light up fluorescent bulbs from a distance. The project was created by our team, with contributions from all members: Manabjit, Sangina, Bhagirath, Priyam, and Jotirmoy.

Plasma Ball

Our Plasma Ball project showcases a visually striking display of plasma and electric currents. The ball contains a mixture of gases, and when electricity is applied, it creates colorful streams of plasma that move in response to touch. It demonstrates concepts of electricity, ionization, and energy transfer. The project was created by our team, including Manabjit, Sangina, Bhagirath, Priyam, and Jotirmoy.

Website

The website, created by Manabjit, serves as a central hub for showcasing our school's Science Exhibition projects. It features detailed information about each exhibit, including our Tesla Coil, Plasma Ball, and other exciting displays. The site also includes updates on the Science Club, highlighting upcoming events, activities, and member contributions. It provides a user-friendly platform to explore all the exhibition details and learn about our school's scientific endeavors.
